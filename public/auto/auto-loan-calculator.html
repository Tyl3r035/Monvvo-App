How to use Monvvo auto loan calculator

  1. Enter the total loan amount for the car you are considering.
  2. Provide the down payment amount, if any, to reduce the principal loan amount.
  3. Select the loan term from the dropdown (e.g., 36, 48, 60 months) to see repayment options.
  4. Input the interest rate offered by your lender based on your credit score.
  5. Add any planned extra monthly payments to see the impact on your loan term and total interest paid.
  6. Click "Update" to calculate your monthly payments, total interest, and loan payoff timeline.
  7. Modify any inputs to explore different loan scenarios and find the best fit for your budget.

Auto Loan Terms

Loan Amount

The total amount financed for the car purchase, minus the down payment and trade-in value.

Down Payment

The upfront amount paid to reduce the loan principal. A larger down payment reduces monthly payments and interest.

Interest Rate

The percentage charged by the lender for the loan. Determined by credit score, loan term, and other factors.

Loan Term

The duration of the loan repayment period, typically ranging from 36 to 84 months.

Extra Payment

Optional additional payments made toward the principal to reduce the loan term and total interest paid.
