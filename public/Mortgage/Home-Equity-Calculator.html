How to Use Monvvo Home Equity Calculator

  1. Enter the Current Home Value.
    Provide the current market value of your home. This amount is essential for the home equity calculator to determine how much equity you have.
  2. Input the Outstanding Mortgage Balance.
    Specify the amount you still owe on your mortgage. This information is used by the mortgage calculator for home equity loan to calculate your available equity.
  3. Add Additional Liens or Loans.
    If you have any additional liens or loans against your home, input those amounts here. This helps the home equity line calculator provide a more accurate estimate of your total equity.
  4. Calculate Your Home Equity.
    Once you have entered all the necessary details, click the “Calculate Home Equity” button to see your home equity amount. This calculation helps you understand how much equity you have built up and what might be available for a home equity loan.

Home Equity Terms

Current Home Value

Current home value is the market value of your property, including any recent appraisals or estimates. This value is a key input for the home equity calculator.

Outstanding Mortgage Balance

Outstanding mortgage balance is the remaining amount you owe on your home mortgage. This value is subtracted from your home’s current value in the mortgage calculator for home equity loan.

Additional Liens or Loans

Additional liens or loans are any other debts secured by your home, such as second mortgages or home equity lines of credit. Including these in the home equity line calculator helps provide an accurate equity calculation.
